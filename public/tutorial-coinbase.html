Step 1

Step 2

Select Settings

Step 3

Click on Bank Account since it's a local bank we are working with

Step 4

When this prompt appears, you just click on Continue.
Always make sure you purchase bank logs that come with email access.

Step 5

Now with Coinbase I recommend you use Chime or Chase Bank log that comes with email access

Step 6

You'll enter the Online Access from the bank log you purchased, send verification code to email and link it instantly
